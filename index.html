Uni High Virtual Community

This website was inspired by Princeton's Winter Session Virtual Community Building website to help keep Uni High's community together while we stay safe from COVID-19. We will try to update this site as frequently as possible with news & activities, so make sure to keep checking in!

Feel free to make any suggestions for things we can add! You are welcome to make content (YouTube tutorials, writing, lists of your favorite books, etc.) to share on here! Unfortunately, we do not have a suggestions button yet, so please contact Emi Brown for now. We also hope to add a calendar soon for scheduled events such as online talks and esports games!
